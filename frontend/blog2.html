Mastering Deep Work

“Deep Work” is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task. Here's how to master it:

  • Block out deep work sessions in your calendar.
  • Eliminate all distractions (notifications, noise).
  • Work in a quiet environment with minimal interruptions.
  • Use noise-canceling headphones or focus music.
  • Train your concentration with regular practice.
  • Set clear goals for each session.
  • Keep your phone out of reach.
  • Reflect on what distracts you and fix it.
