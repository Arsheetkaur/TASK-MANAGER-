Pomodoro: Work Smart

The Pomodoro Technique helps boost productivity with focused bursts of work. Here's how it works:

  • Choose a task to work on.
  • Set a timer for 25 minutes (1 Pomodoro).
  • Work on the task until the timer rings.
  • Take a 5-minute break.
  • Repeat the process 4 times, then take a longer break (15–30 minutes).

Pomodoro improves time awareness, prevents burnout, and trains your brain to focus. Try using a Pomodoro timer tool today!
