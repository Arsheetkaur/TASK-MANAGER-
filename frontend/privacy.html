Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us!

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide TaskFlow’s services, including your name, email, and task-related information when you sign up or use our features.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to personalize your experience, improve our services, and send optional notifications (if enabled). We never sell your data.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we strive to keep your information safe.

4. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your data via your profile. Contact us at support@taskflow.com for assistance.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Check this page for the latest version (last updated April 2025).

For questions, reach out via our contact page.
