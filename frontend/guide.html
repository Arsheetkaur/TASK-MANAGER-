TaskFlow User Guide

Get started with TaskFlow!

1. Sign Up or Log In

Create an account via signup or log in at login to access all features.

2. Create and Manage Tasks

Use the Tasks page to add, edit, and complete tasks. Set due dates and priorities to stay organized.

3. Boost Focus with Pomodoro

Try the Pomodoro Timer to work in focused intervals and maintain productivity.

4. Collaborate with Your Team

Invite team members via the Collaboration feature to share tasks and track progress.

5. Track Your Progress

Visit Analytics to review task completion rates and productivity trends.

6. Explore Productivity Tips

Check out our blog for tips like “Top 10 Productivity Hacks” and “Mastering Deep Work.”

7. Customize Your Profile

Update your details and settings in your Profile to personalize your experience.

Need help? Visit our contact page or explore more on the homepage.
