How to Use TaskFlow

  • To-Do List: Add, delete, mark done, and prioritize your tasks.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Use this to boost productivity using 25/5 minute cycles.
  • Stats: See how many tasks you've completed and your time spent focused.
  • Settings: Customize your experience, from themes to timer length.

Tips

  • Break large tasks into smaller sub-tasks.
  • Use dark mode at night to reduce eye strain.
  • Enable sound in settings to get notified when a Pomodoro ends.
